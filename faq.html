Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Clear answers about trading apps, demat accounts, and crypto in India.

General

Which is the best trading app in India for beginners? +
Groww is best for beginners due to its simple UI. Angel One is suitable for active traders.
How can I open a demat account online? +
You can open a demat account online on Groww or Angel One using PAN, Aadhaar, and bank details.
Can I start investing with small amounts? +
Yes, you can start with small amounts using SIPs or small stock investments.

Crypto

Is crypto trading legal in India? +
Yes, crypto trading is legal in India, but it involves high risk and taxes apply.
Which crypto app is best for beginners? +
CoinDCX is beginner-friendly with INR deposits and easy UI.
Is futures trading safe? +
Futures trading is risky and recommended only for experienced traders.

Security

How do I keep my account secure? +
Enable 2FA, use strong passwords, and never share OTPs.
