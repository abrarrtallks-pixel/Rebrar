How to Open a Groww Demat Account (Step-by-Step)

This beginner-friendly guide shows how to open a Groww demat account and start investing in stocks, ETFs, and mutual funds (SIP) in India.

Open Free Demat on Groww

Step-by-Step: Groww Account Opening

  1. Download the Groww app from Play Store/App Store.
  2. Sign up with your mobile number or email.
  3. Complete KYC using PAN, Aadhaar, and bank details.
  4. Upload documents and verify OTP.
  5. Account activation usually takes minutes to 24 hours.
  6. Start investing in stocks, ETFs, and SIPs.

Documents needed: PAN, Aadhaar, bank account.
