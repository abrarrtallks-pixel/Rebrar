How to Trade Crypto Futures on Delta Exchange (Step-by-Step)

This guide is for experienced traders who want to trade crypto futures & derivatives on Delta Exchange. Futures trading is high-risk—use strict risk management.

Trade on Delta Exchange

Step-by-Step: Delta Exchange Setup

  1. Create an account on Delta Exchange.
  2. Complete identity verification.
  3. Deposit USDT/crypto to your wallet.
  4. Select futures contracts and set leverage carefully.
  5. Use stop-loss and take-profit orders.
  6. Monitor PnL and manage positions.

Risk Management Tips

  • Never use high leverage as a beginner.
  • Risk only a small % per trade.
  • Always place stop-loss.
  • Avoid emotional trading.
Start Futures Trading

Who Should Use Delta Exchange?

Delta Exchange is suitable for traders who already understand technical analysis, leverage, and risk management. Beginners should start with spot trading first.

⚠️ Futures trading involves significant risk and may result in losses.
