How to Open a Demat Account on Groww (Step-by-Step)

  1. Download the Groww app from Play Store/App Store.
  2. Sign up using your mobile number or email.
  3. Complete KYC with PAN, Aadhaar, and bank details.
  4. Upload required documents and verify OTP.
  5. Account activation usually completes within minutes to 24 hours.
  6. Start investing in stocks, ETFs, and mutual funds (SIP).

Best for: Beginners & long-term investors.

How to Start Trading with Angel One

  1. Install the Angel One app or visit the official website.
  2. Create an account with your mobile number/email.
  3. Complete KYC (PAN, Aadhaar, bank details).
  4. Select trading segments: equity, intraday, options & futures.
  5. Wait for account activation and fund your account.
  6. Use charts and research tools to place trades.

Best for: Active traders.

Open Trading Account on Angel One

How to Buy Crypto on CoinDCX (Spot Trading)

  1. Download the CoinDCX app or visit the website.
  2. Register with email/mobile number.
  3. Complete KYC verification.
  4. Add INR funds using UPI or bank transfer.
  5. Buy popular crypto assets (BTC, ETH, etc.).
  6. Enable 2FA to secure your account.

Best for: Crypto beginners.

How to Trade Crypto Futures on Delta Exchange

  1. Sign up on Delta Exchange website/app.
  2. Complete identity verification.
  3. Deposit USDT/crypto.
  4. Select futures contracts and set leverage carefully.
  5. Use stop-loss and risk management.
  6. Monitor PnL and manage positions.

Best for: Experienced traders only.
