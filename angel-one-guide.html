How to Open an Angel One Trading & Demat Account

This guide explains how to open an Angel One trading account for intraday, options, and futures trading in India.

Open Trading Account on Angel One

Step-by-Step: Angel One Account Opening

  1. Install the Angel One app or visit the official website.
  2. Register with your mobile number or email.
  3. Complete KYC (PAN, Aadhaar, bank details).
  4. Select trading segments: equity, intraday, options & futures.
  5. Verify documents and fund your account.
  6. Use charts & research tools to place your first trade.

Documents needed: PAN, Aadhaar, bank account.

Why Choose Angel One?

  • Advanced tools for active traders
  • Options & futures trading
  • Research reports & pro charts
  • Fast order execution
