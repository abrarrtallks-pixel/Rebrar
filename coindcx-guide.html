How to Create a CoinDCX Account & Buy Crypto (Step-by-Step)

This guide shows you how to open a CoinDCX account and start spot crypto trading in India. Ideal for beginners who want to buy and hold crypto.

Create CoinDCX Account

Step-by-Step: CoinDCX Account Setup

  1. Download the CoinDCX app or visit the official website.
  2. Register using your email or mobile number.
  3. Complete KYC verification.
  4. Add funds via INR (UPI/bank transfer).
  5. Buy popular cryptocurrencies (BTC, ETH, etc.).
  6. Enable 2FA to secure your account.

Tip: Start with small amounts and learn basics first.

Why Choose CoinDCX?

  • INR deposits & withdrawals
  • Wide range of crypto assets
  • Easy UI & portfolio tracking
